What Data We Process

The short version: almost nothing leaves your phone. GPS processing happens entirely on-device. Only a coarse geographic area (not your precise location) is sent to retrieve camera data.

1. On-device GPS data

Your GPS coordinates are processed on your device in real time to measure speed against camera locations. Your precise coordinates are never transmitted to our servers.

2. Approximate location for camera data

To fetch camera locations, the App sends a coarse bounding box (~111 km wide) to OpenStreetMap's Overpass API. No identifying information is included and this cannot be linked back to you.

3. Trip summaries (local storage only)

If you save a trip, the following is stored locally via Apple's UserDefaults and never leaves your device:

  • Start and end time
  • Distance, duration, max and average speed
  • Cameras passed (location, speed limit, your recorded speed, over-limit flag)
4. Data we do not collect
  • Your name, email, or any identity information
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA) or cross-app tracking
  • Precise GPS history sent to our servers
  • Crash reports or analytics (no third-party SDK integrated)

Legal Basis (UK & EU GDPR)

  • Bounding-box requests to Overpass API — Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)). Only a coarse rectangle with no user identifier is sent.
  • On-device GPS processing — Performance of the service you requested (Art. 6(1)(b)).
  • Local trip storage — Your consent, given by choosing to save a trip. Withdraw at any time via Settings → Clear All History (Art. 6(1)(a)).

Third-Party Services

OpenStreetMap / Overpass API

Camera location requests are made to independently operated Overpass API servers. Each request contains only a geographic bounding box and a standard User-Agent string ("DriveSafeApp/1.0"). No personal data or device identifiers are sent. Camera data is cached on-device for up to 7 days.

Apple (iOS Platform)

The App is distributed via the Apple App Store. Apple may collect diagnostic and usage data per Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not control or access this data.

Data Retention

  • On-device trip data — Retained until you delete it (Settings → Clear All History) or uninstall the App.
  • Camera tile cache — Automatically purged after 7 days.
  • Overpass API server logs — Managed by respective server operators; we have no access.

Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored trip data from your device.

International Data Transfers

Bounding-box requests to Overpass API mirrors may be processed on servers outside the UK and EEA (including Germany, Austria, and Russia). Data transferred is non-personal in nature — a geographic rectangle with no user identifier.
